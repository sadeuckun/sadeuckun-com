About

Hey, this is Safa -- a software engineer based in Konya, Türkiye. I studied computer engineering at Boğaziçi University and have been making a living creating software for more than a decade.

Being always interested in all sorts of technology, I am an aspiring hacker inside.

I'm currently looking for my next Engineering Manager role. Most recently, I led engineering at RTIC Outdoors, building products around warehouse management, mobile device management, and retail — while also building a community of local hackers and a highly functional community space for Konya Hackerspace.

When I have some free time, I like to read non-fiction books, do hobby electronics or make fun videos for YouTube.
