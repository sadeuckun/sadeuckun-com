About

I'm Sade, a software developer based in Samsun, Turkiye. I build CRM systems, management panels, accounting software, tracking systems and mobile applications for teams that need practical, scalable software.

My work focuses on turning complex operational needs into reliable products. I care about performance, sustainability and clean system design, and I use AI as a tool to strengthen production workflows rather than as a shortcut.

Alongside application development, I actively work on Linux servers, self-hosted systems, CI/CD pipelines, security, deployment and monitoring. I manage my own infrastructure and use it as a hands-on environment for building more stable and maintainable systems.

I also spend a lot of time on automation and process optimization through n8n, AI tools and custom integrations. Across health, finance and business operations, I build systems that help teams move faster, make better decisions and reduce operational friction.
